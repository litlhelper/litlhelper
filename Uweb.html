Help for Flood Disaster

The people of Valencia need your help. Any donation is important, especially for children and the elderly. Your support can make a difference.

Your support can make a difference.

It is estimated that approximately 30 billion euros will be needed to restore and recover the entire flooded area. Several towns have been affected, with many families seeing their homes demolished due to structural damage.

The pain of the many people who have lost their lives in the disaster is immense.

New schools, nursing homes, centers for people with special needs, daycare centers, health centers, and the necessary medical supplies will need to be restored or rebuilt, among other infrastructures.

Any donation, no matter how small, can make a big difference in the lives of those affected by this tragedy. Thank you for your generosity and support.

Funds received will be sent to organizations such as the Red Cross (Cruz Roja) and small local organizations dedicated to recovery efforts in the affected areas.

Thanks a lot for your generosity and support.
